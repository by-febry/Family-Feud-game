Family Feud Game

In Family Feud, two teams compete to guess the most popular answers to survey questions posed to 100 people. Each round begins with a face-off where one member from each team tries to provide the top answer to the question. The team that wins the face-off can either play the round or pass it to the opposing team. The playing team then attempts to guess the remaining answers on the board. If they get three wrong answers, the opposing team gets a chance to steal the points by guessing any remaining answer. The game continues until one team reaches a predetermined point total to win.
